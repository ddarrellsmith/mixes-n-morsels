Morsels and Mixes

This website will work as a community-contributed catalog of recipes. It’s a one stop shop to find recipes for snickerdoodle cookies, a half-dozen hot cocoa variations, tiramisu, and the espresso martini. 

 

This website will allow for viewing, creating, and searching for recipes for recipes for snacks and mixed drinks. The home page will show the user a prominent search bar, an algorithmically generated pairing of a drink and a snack, and tile buttons for viewing various groupings of recipes (all snacks, sweet snacks, rum-based cocktails, etc.).

 

The “catalog” page will show recipe cards (showing a picture, recipe name, author, brief description, and keywords) in a bootstrap grid and allow for sorting by category [drink, snack], keywords, author, and ingredients. Clicking on a recipe will launch into a recipe-specific page which will show the information in the recipe card as well as the full ingredients list and preparation instructions.

 

Lastly, users will be able to navigate to a “Submit Recipe” page. Separate submission elements will be present for the Recipe Title, Author Name, Category, Keywords, Description, Ingredients, and Preparation Instructions. Users will also be able to upload an image of the recipe as well. Submitted recipes will be stored to the website host and viewable within the recipe catalog.
